My Professional Activities (Last updated September 2025)

Reviewing Services

For an up-to-date and verified list of my reviewing activities, please visit my ORCID profile > Peer review section.

I have had the opportunity to review papers for the following conferences and journals:

  • International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR): Reviewed papers on deep learning.

  • Transactions on Machine Learning Research: Reviewed papers on representation learning and robustness.

  • Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence: Reviewed works focused on application of Artificial Intelligence.
  • Artificial Intelligence in Medicine: Reviewed papers focused on application of Artificial Intelligence in Medicine.
  • npj Digital Medicine: Reviewed papers focused on digital technologies (e.g. machine learning) in medicine and healthcare. Impact Factor: 15.357, 5-year Journal Impact Factor: 17.0 (2024)
  • Scientific Reports Journal: Reviewed papers focused on Neuroscience, psychology and Artificial Intelligence.
  • NeuroImage Journal: Reviewed papers focused on cognitive psychology and Artificial Intelligence.

  • Cognitive Computation Journal: Reviewed papers focused on cognitive psychology and Artificial Intelligence.

  • Workshops at Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS): Reviewed works focused on Artificial Intelligence, Creativity & Generative AI, Causality and Large Models and Time Series Analysis.
  • Workshop at International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR): Reviewed works focused on Artificial Intelligence and Finance for the ICLR 2025 Workshop Advances in Financial AI workshop.
